What happens after you send

  • Your theme is uploaded securely and a review request is opened for the maintainers.
  • They check that files are safe and the theme structure looks good.
  • Brand-new folder names can often be merged automatically after checks pass.
  • If the same folder name already exists, we do not auto-merge — a person will look at your PR first so nothing is overwritten by surprise.
  • Once accepted, your theme appears on this site for everyone to download.
