Add your theme to the gallery

Share a new look for the Innioasis Y1 player. Choose a ZIP file or a whole theme folder — we’ll package it safely in your browser and send it to the team for review.

You’ll get a link to track your submission. New themes are usually quick to review; if your folder name matches one we already have, a maintainer will confirm everything before it goes live.

One theme per ZIP is easiest. Don’t pick both a ZIP and a folder.
Select the folder that contains your theme’s config.json and images. Works in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

What happens after you send

  • Your theme is uploaded securely and a review request is opened for the maintainers.
  • They check that files are safe and the theme structure looks good.
  • Brand-new folder names can often be merged automatically after checks pass.
  • If the same folder name already exists, we do not auto-merge — a person will look at your PR first so nothing is overwritten by surprise.
  • Once accepted, your theme appears on this site for everyone to download.
