Upload your theme

Share a new look for the Innioasis Y1 player. Choose a ZIP file or a whole theme folder — we’ll package it safely in your browser and send it to the team.

You’ll get a link to track your submission. Only brand-new theme identities may qualify for automatic submission after our checks; edits, anything that still needs a maintainer after automatic checks, and removals always go through someone on the team before they appear on the site.

    What happens after you send

    • Your theme is uploaded securely and a submission is queued for the maintainers.
    • They check that files are safe and the theme structure looks good.
    • Brand-new folder names can often go through automatic submission once our checks pass.
    • If the same folder name already exists, we do not use automatic submission — someone on the team will review your submission first so nothing is overwritten by surprise.
    • Once accepted, your theme appears on this site for everyone to download.
