How we decide

Gold is a community quality mark, not a warranty. We automatically check each theme so the badge matches what is in the package. If a future Y1 update breaks something, the badge can come off until it is fixed.

Technical details (for theme makers)

A theme earns Gold when it passes our artwork compatibility check (same as the gallery badge):

  • config.json lists paths for the Rockbox launcher switch, eBook reader, calculator, and calendar.
  • Each linked file exists and is real artwork — not transparent.png placeholders on launcher.png, calendar.png, ebook.png, or calculator.png.
  • Authors may set gallery.compatibilityMedal to gold or none to force the badge on or off.

Themes are checked in batches when you open this page or the gallery. Brand-new uploads may take a moment before Gold appears.

Always back up your Y1 before installing any theme.
