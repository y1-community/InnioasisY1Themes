NOTICE

Due to recent hardware changes to the Y1, Rockbox-Y1 is not compatible with Y1s that came with OS 3.0.7 out of the box and will break your device. If this has happened to you please try installing 3.0.7 in Updater, and if this does not work, please contact Innioasis for assistance. Existing Users of devices running Rockbox-Y1 are not affected.
